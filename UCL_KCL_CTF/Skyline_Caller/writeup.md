# Skyline Caller - CTF Challenge Writeup

## Challenge Description
During a phone call, an image of a city skyline was shared. Identify the tallest building shown and determine its height.

**Flag format:** `flag{buildingname_height}`

## Solution

### Step 1: Image Analysis
The provided image (`pic_osint_1.jpg`) shows a distinctive modern skyline featuring a tall twisted skyscraper next to a waterfront. The architectural design is immediately recognizable due to its unique twisted facade.

### Step 2: Building Identification
The building in the image is **Karlatornet** (meaning "The Karla Tower" in Swedish), located in Gothenburg, Sweden. Key identifying features:

- **Twisted facade design**: The building has a distinctive twisting architectural style
- **Location**: Waterfront setting in the Lindholmen district of Gothenburg
- **Modern glass construction**: The dark glass exterior with geometric patterns
- **Surrounding area**: The mix of modern office buildings and residential apartments matches the Gothenburg harbor area

### Step 3: Height Verification
Karlatornet stands at **246 meters (807 feet)** tall with 74 floors. It holds the record as:
- The tallest building in Scandinavia
- The tallest building in the Nordic countries
- One of the tallest residential buildings in Europe

The building was designed by Skidmore, Owings and Merrill (SOM) and was completed in 2024.

### Step 4: Flag Construction
Based on the flag format `flag{buildingname_height}`:
- Building name: karlatornet
- Height: 246m

## Summary

**Original Image:** A photograph of the Gothenburg, Sweden skyline featuring Karlatornet

**Building Identified:** Karlatornet (The Karla Tower)

**Height:** 246 meters
